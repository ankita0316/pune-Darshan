Pune is the 8th largest metropolis in India. It is on the Deccan Plateau in the Indian state of Maharashtra. The city is famous for the great Maratha king Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. It is also the birthplace of spiritual teacher Meher Baba. Mutha are the two rivers that flow through the city. It has a major Information Technology (IT) center. This city is managed by the Pune Municipal Council (PMC). Pune is a city of knowledge and often known as the Oxford of the east. In Marathi, it is known as vidyeche maherghar. It means the hometown of education. The population of Pune was 8,242,142 by 2015.Pune is surrounded by scenic hills and forts, such as Sinhagad, Rajgad, and Shivneri, that offer stunning views and adventure opportunities. It is also close to the hill stations of Lonavala, Khandala, and Mahabaleshwar.Pune is a city that blends tradition and modernity, heritage and innovation, culture and commerce. It is a city that welcomes visitors with its warmth and charm.

Sinhagad is an ancient hill fortress located at around 49 km southwest of the city of Pune, India .
